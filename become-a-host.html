What We Look For

A safe, warm, welcoming home

A private furnished bedroom The student's own room with a bed, desk, dresser, and closet space.

Common Questions

Host family FAQs

How much will I earn as a host family?

Stipend amounts vary by program length and location. Our team will discuss the current rate with you during the application process. Payments are made on a consistent monthly schedule.

Do I need a spare room or can I convert one?

The room needs to be private and reasonably sized. It doesn't need to be a traditional guest room, but it must have a door that closes, adequate lighting, and space for a desk and storage. Our home inspector can advise during the visit.

Can I choose which student I host?

We do the matching based on your preferences and student profiles, but you can share your preferences — such as gender, age range, dietary needs, or language. We always aim to find the best fit for both parties.

What if there's an issue with the student?

USH provides ongoing support for both hosts and students. If any issues arise, contact our team directly during business hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm PST) and we'll assist immediately. You're never on your own.

How long do students typically stay?

Program lengths vary from a few weeks to a full academic year. We'll match you with a student whose timeline fits yours. Many hosts choose to host consecutive students year-round.
