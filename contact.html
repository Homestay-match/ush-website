How long does the placement process take?
Most students receive their host family match within 5–10 business days after submitting their application and payment.
Can I request a specific type of host family?
Yes. During the application you can specify preferences such as no pets, no children, female host, proximity to school, and dietary requirements. We do our best to match accordingly.
What is included in the homestay fee?
All homestays include a private furnished room, use of common areas, Wi-Fi, and laundry access. Meal plans (breakfast & dinner, or continental) are available as an add-on.
Do you work with overseas agents?
Absolutely. We partner with 700+ agencies in more than 50 countries. Visit our Partners page to learn more or get in touch to start a partnership.
How do I become a host family?
Send us a message using the contact form and select "Becoming a host family" as the topic. Our team will guide you through our 98-point home verification process.
