📋Overview

This policy outlines the charges and refunds that apply when guests cancel unconfirmed or confirmed homestay matches. All cancellations must be submitted via email to the provided USH contact address.

USH reserves the right to modify charges based on circumstances, with final decision-making authority on all disputes and refunds.

Important: The Application Fee and Security Deposit are never refunded under any cancellation scenario. Refunds exclude merchant fees and wire transfer fees (a $65 deduction applies if a wire transfer was used).

📅Before Move-In Cancellations

The following table outlines what fees apply when a cancellation occurs before the guest moves into the accommodation.

Scenario Application Fee Security Deposit Housing Fee
Not yet confirmed (before host acceptance) No Refund No Refund No Charge
30+ days before move-in date No Refund No Refund 100% Refund
11–29 days before move-in date No Refund No Refund 2 Weeks Payment
10 days or less before move-in (including no-show) No Refund No Refund 4 Weeks Payment

🏠After Move-In Cancellations

Once a guest has moved into the accommodation, a minimum notice period is required for cancellation.

Scenario Application Fee Security Deposit Housing Fee
Written 30-day notice required No Refund No Refund 30-day minimum + additional days; any excess refunded

30-Day Notice Rule: After moving in, you must provide at least 30 days written notice to cancel. You will be charged for those 30 days plus any additional days beyond your notice date. Any housing fees already paid beyond that period will be refunded.

📌Notes & Additional Fees

Cancellation Fee: USH reserves the right to charge either $225 or 10% of the refund amount, whichever is greater. This fee is deducted from any remaining housing fee refund.

Merchant & Wire Transfer Fees: All refunds exclude merchant processing fees. If payment was made via wire transfer, an additional $65 deduction applies.

How to Cancel: All cancellations must be submitted in writing via email to USH. Cancellations communicated only to your Host will not be considered valid.

Refund Timeline: Any available refund will be returned to the same credit card within 21 days of the cancellation being processed.

USH Discretion: USH reserves the right to modify charges and refund amounts based on individual circumstances. USH has final decision-making authority on all disputes and refunds.

Host Cancellations: If a Host cancels your confirmed match, please contact USH directly. Refund terms for Host-initiated cancellations are handled at USH's discretion.
