Last Updated: February 9, 2020

This Guest Cancellation Policy outlines the charges and refunds for Guests when an unconfirmed Match (Homestay Application) or confirmed Match is cancelled by the Guest. A reservation can be cancelled by emailing support@ushstudent.com. USH will use reasonable efforts to resolve any dispute or problem between a Guest and a Host, and USH reserves the right to modify the amounts a Guest is required to pay based on specific circumstances. USH has the final decision on all disputes, problems, charges and refunds, and USH's decision will be binding on the Guest. If you have any comments or questions about this Guest Cancellation Policy, feel free to contact us via email at support@ushstudent.com.

Homestay Confirmed Guest cancels Booking 10 days or less before move in, including no show No Refund No Refund Guest pays for 4 weeks**

Status Application Fee Security Deposit Homestay / Housing Fee
