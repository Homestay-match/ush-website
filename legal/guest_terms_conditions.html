  • Security Deposit – this is a fee collected from each student and will be retained by USH until the completion of the student's housing contract (security deposit is not refundable in case of cancellation after homestay matching services has begun). To ensure return of the full amount, please be sure to settle any of the above financial responsibilities with your host. USH will return the security deposit after confirming with your Host to verify the amount of refund due to you after 30 days from your departure from your homestay.   • Convenience Fee – this is the fee paid for the convenience of using a credit card. A 5.80% convenience fee will be applied to all credit card payments. The convenience fee may vary.
  • Taxes – where applicable, USH will collect required taxes.
  • Total Fee – this is the total of all of the above amounts, which a Guest is charged when a Host accepts a Homestay Application.

  • Wire Transfer Fee: Guests who choose to make payment via wire transfer must contact USH for the bank information. Wire transfers require a $55 bank fee and must be added to the amount due to USH for each wire transfer.

Other Potential Fees:

  • Airport Transfer Fee – this is the fee charged by USH for an airport shuttle transfer to the Accommodation, as further described in Section 8(c) below. If you requested airport transfer, this fee will be included on your housing fee invoice. You must pay for your airport shuttle fee along with your housing fee.
  • Late Fee – this is the fee a Guest may pay if their payment is not received before the required due date. A Late Fee may be applied on all required payments, including original Matches, extensions and change of reservation dates.
  • An additional US $150.00 late fee will be charged if your reservation is made less than 15 days prior to arrival.
