How It Works

Find your homestay in 3 simple steps

01

Search by School

Select your university, set your dates, and browse verified homestays near campus. Filter by price, room type, meal plan, and more.

02

Compare & Choose

View real photos, read host profiles, check room amenities, and see exactly where you'll be living. Every listing shows distance to campus.

03

Book with Confidence

Every Listing Includes

Transparency you can count on

We believe you should see exactly where you are living before you arrive. Every detail on our platform is verified by a USH team member to ensure your home matches your expectations.

Real Photos

See actual photos of the home, your bedroom, and common areas taken by our team during our in-person inspection.

Host Profiles

Get to know your host family, their interests, experience hosting, and house rules.

Room Amenities

See exactly what your room includes, from private essentials like a desk, dresser, and closet to details on shared or private bathrooms.

Distance to Campus

Know exactly how far your homestay is from school with transit options and commute times.

Meal Plans

Choose from room only, breakfast, or full meal plan options offered by each host.

Verified Reviews

Read feedback from previous students who stayed in the same home.
