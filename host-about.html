Connecting International Students with Host Families Since 1998

For over 27 years, USH has been connecting welcoming American families with international students. We are one of the few companies that still personally visits every home and hand-selects every match.

Our Story

Built from Experience, Driven by Mission

In 1998, finding verified housing from abroad was nearly impossible, leaving students vulnerable to uncertainty. Decades later, the landscape has changed, but the risk remains the same. While many platforms offer thousands of unverified listings, most never step foot in the homes they advertise.

We started USH to change that. We believe that an international student's focus should be on their education, not the stress of unverified housing. We are one of the few companies that still prioritizes the "Human Standard" — personally visiting every home, meeting every host, and hand-selecting every match.

Nearly 30 years later, that mission has not changed. We remain a human-led company dedicated to providing the same high-quality vetting and support we would want for our own family.

Why Host with USH

What makes us different

100% In-Person Verification

A USH ambassador visits your home for a brief 15-minute meeting to answer your questions and take photos of the student's room and common areas. This in-person visit ensures our quality standards are met and provides the best support from the start.

Full-Service Matching

Our team handles all the heavy lifting to find the right student for you. We manage the outreach, answer your questions, and coordinate all logistics so you don't have to. We work to find a compatible match for your home.

Dedicated Support

You are never hosting alone. Our housing coordinators are available to provide guidance and help with any adjustments needed. If anything arises, our team is here to provide you guidance.

Host Payment Coordination

We coordinate all student payments on your behalf, so you never have to request payment directly. While payments are made by students, our team manages billing, communication, and extensions to keep everything running smoothly and professionally.
