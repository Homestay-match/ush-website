HomestayMatch

Another way to experience a USH homestay

HomestayMatch is our sister platform that gives agents, schools, and students visibility and transparency into our quality. For students, it provides the freedom to browse and choose their own verified homestay.

Every listing features host profiles and original photos of the home and room. All photos are taken by our team during our in-person inspections. This ensures students see exactly where they will be living and who their host will be before they arrive.
