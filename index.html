Every Home Is Personally Verified

Our team visits every home in person to complete a full evaluation of the house and bedroom. We interview every host family to ensure they are committed to supporting international students, not just providing a room. Only homes that meet our high-quality standards are approved.

Verification includes in-person visits, host interviews, background checks, and ongoing assessments.
