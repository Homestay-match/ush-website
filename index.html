Your Safety, Our Standard

Every Home Is Personally Verified

Our team visits every home in person, meets every host, and completes a full home and bedroom evaluation to ensure student comfort before approval. We also interview every host family to confirm they genuinely support international students, not just offer a room. Only homes that meet our standards are approved.

Verification includes in-person visits, host interviews, background checks, neighborhood evaluations, and ongoing assessments.
