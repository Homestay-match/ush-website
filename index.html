How It Works

How to Apply for International Student Housing USA — 3 Simple Steps

01

Apply Online (3 Minutes or less)

Tell us about yourself, your school, and your preferences. Submit your application in just a few minutes, and we'll send you a confirmation email right away.

02

We Find Your Perfect Match

Our team reviews your application and matches you with a verified host family near your campus. Once confirmed and your housing fee is paid, you'll receive your welcome package with host details, a homestay guide, your bus route, and an orientation video.

03

Arrive and Feel at Home

We help coordinate your move-in with your host family so you know your host, where you will live, and how to get to school. We also check in after 72 hours to make sure everything is going well.
