Application Received!

Thank you for taking the first step toward becoming a host. We are thrilled you've chosen to share your home and culture with an international student. Our team is looking forward to meeting you!

What Happens Next

1

Welcome Call

Within the next 24 to 48 business hours, a member of our team will call you to chat about your home and answer any questions you have about the program.

2

The 15-Minute Home Visit

We'll schedule a quick, 15-minute visit to meet you and finalize your application. During this time, we will take professional photos of the student's space, so please ensure the bedroom is fully furnished and ready for its close-up!

3

The Perfect Match

Once approved, we will begin looking for the right student who fits your hosting preferences and lifestyle.
