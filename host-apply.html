House Rules
Meals

Room only: Student is responsible for their own meals. You will not be responsible for preparing meals — student should have kitchen privileges.

Breakfast only: You only have to make breakfast foods available each morning. You do not need to prepare them.

Rooms with meals: Includes a continental breakfast and a second meal of the day (usually dinner) that you will prepare for the student.

Student Preferences

Single: You have a bedroom that can accommodate one student.

Double 1 Bed: Your bedroom can accommodate 2 students who request to share a bed (e.g., sisters attending the same school).

Double 2 Bed: Your bedroom has two beds for 2 students who request to share one room with separate beds.

Authorization of Background Check

By checking the box below, you authorize USH to screen you for criminal and sex offenses that may confirm or deny your eligibility for hosting students in your home. What happens next

1

Within the next 24–48 business hours, a USH Representative will contact you to go over the details of our program.

2

We'll schedule a Home Visit — a USH representative will meet you in person and view the room you have available.

3

Once approved, we'll match you with a student based on your preferences and welcome them to your home.

Hosting is a great opportunity for cultural exchange, making new friendships, and earning extra monthly income. We look forward to working with you!
