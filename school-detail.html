About This Location

Homestay for International Students near UCLA Extension

UCLA Extension is located in the Westwood neighborhood of Los Angeles, one of the most desirable areas in the city. Our host families near UCLA Extension live in safe, residential neighborhoods in Westwood, Brentwood, Santa Monica, and surrounding communities, all within convenient reach of campus by public transit or a short drive.

Students enjoy easy access to shops, restaurants, parks, and cultural attractions. The area is well connected by Metro bus lines and the Expo Line, making it simple to get to campus and explore the greater Los Angeles area.
