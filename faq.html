Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about homestay placements, pricing, and what to expect before you arrive.

Categories
General
A homestay is a private room in a local family's home. You live with a host family who provides a safe, welcoming environment and helps you experience American culture firsthand. Meals may be included depending on your plan.
Universal Student Housing (USH) has been placing international students with verified host families in the United States since 1998 — over 27 years of experience. Every match is made by a real person, not an algorithm.
We have host families near schools in Southern California, Northern California, Arizona, Florida, and New York. Visit our pricing page to see schools and availability in each region.
We partner with over 270 schools and universities across the US. Check our schools list or contact us if you don't see your school listed.
Pricing & Payment
Pricing varies by region and meal plan. Rates start from $850/month in Arizona and up to $1,200/month in New York. Unlike other companies, we show our prices upfront — no hidden fees. See full pricing on our pricing page.
There is a one-time placement fee to cover the cost of matching, verifying, and coordinating your homestay. All fees are disclosed clearly before you apply — no surprises.
We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is processed securely online. Contact us if you need assistance with international payment methods.
Placement Process
After you apply (takes about 3 minutes), our team reviews your preferences — location, school, meal plan, move-in date — and personally matches you with a verified host family nearby. We consider personality, lifestyle, and cultural fit.
We recommend applying at least 4–6 weeks before your move-in date. Popular locations near campus fill up quickly, especially at the start of each semester. The earlier you apply, the more options you'll have.
Yes! If you prefer to browse and select your own host, you can do so through our sister platform HomestayMatch, where you can view host profiles, photos, and commute times before booking.
Living in a Homestay
A private furnished bedroom, shared bathroom, access to common areas (kitchen, living room), and Wi-Fi. Meal plans (breakfast only, or breakfast + dinner) are available depending on the host family and region.
We offer a 72-Hour Satisfaction Guarantee. If you're not satisfied within your first 72 hours, contact us and we'll help you find a new host family. Your comfort and safety is our priority.
Yes, many students stay with their host families for a full academic year or longer. Just let us know your intended duration when applying and we'll match you accordingly.
Safety & Verification
Every home is visited in person by our team before being approved. Our verification covers 98 different factors including home safety, bedroom quality, bathroom access, neighborhood, and a personal interview with the host family.
Yes. All host families pass our comprehensive in-person verification and background checks. We have been placing students safely since 1998, with over 19,000 students hosted across the US.
Our support team is available throughout your entire stay — from the day you apply to move-out day. You can reach us by phone, email, or through your student portal. We respond to urgent issues quickly.
