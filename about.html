Our Story

Why we started, and why it still matters

Our founder came to the United States as an international student and experienced firsthand what it felt like to arrive in a new country without reliable housing, support, or anyone to guide them. Housing options were difficult to verify from abroad, and students were too often placed in homes that did not meet their expectations or needs.

In 1998, he started USH to solve that problem. Because finding a place to live in America without credit history, local knowledge, or fluent English is overwhelming. The risks are real, the costs are high, and the process is confusing for someone who is thousands of miles from home.

Students need more than just a place to stay. They need verified housing, a welcoming host family who makes them feel at home, and a team that supports them every step of the way, from move-in to move-out.

Nearly 30 years later that mission has not changed. We personally meet every host, visit every home, and rely on a team trained to ensure each student is matched with the right family and supported every step of the way.

The USH Advantage

What makes us different

Over 25 years of experience

Since 1998, we have focused exclusively on supporting international students and the institutions that serve them. Over time, we have developed a strong understanding of what makes a successful homestay experience. This experience guides every placement we make and helps ensure a smooth and reliable process.

Every home personally verified

Every host family is carefully interviewed, and each home is visited by a member of our team before approval. We do not rely on listings or self-reported information. This allows us to maintain clear standards and ensures students know what to expect when they arrive.

Full-service matching

We handle the entire placement process from start to finish, making it simple and clear for students. Each application is reviewed in detail, and we personally match every student with a host family based on their preferences, school location, and lifestyle. Students do not need to search on their own or make decisions without guidance.

Consistent quality, every placement

Our structured process ensures the same high standard across all locations and lengths of stay. Whether a student stays for a few weeks or several months, the experience remains consistent and dependable. Students and partners can rely on a clear, well-managed housing process.
