Our Story

Built from Experience, Driven by Mission

In 1998, finding verified housing from abroad was nearly impossible, leaving students vulnerable to uncertainty. Decades later, the landscape has changed, but the risk remains the same. While many modern platforms offer thousands of unverified listings, most never step foot in the homes they advertise. Students are often left to navigate a sea of digital photos and "self-reported" information, leading to high-cost mistakes and a lack of support when it's needed most.

We started USH to change that. We believe that an international student's focus should be on their education, not the stress of unverified housing. We are one of the few companies that still prioritizes the "Human Standard" — personally visiting every home, meeting every host, and hand-selecting every match.

Nearly 30 years later, that mission has not changed. We remain a human-led company dedicated to providing the same high-quality vetting and support we would want for our own family.

The USH Difference

What makes us different

27+ Years of Expertise

Since 1998, we have specialized exclusively in international student housing. We understand the unique needs of students, parents, overseas agents, and our campus partners, making the transition to the U.S. seamless and reliable for everyone involved.

100% In-Person Verification

We do not rely on stock photos or self-reported listings. A USH coordinator visits every home in person to interview the host and inspect the accommodations. This rigorous vetting process ensures that every placement meets our high-quality standards before a student ever arrives.

Human-Led Matching

We believe the best matches are made by people, not algorithms. Our placement team hand-selects host families based on specific lifestyle preferences, school location, and commute needs, providing a personalized experience that automated platforms simply cannot match.

Personal Support & Satisfaction

Our commitment to our partners and students continues long after move-in. We offer a 72-hour satisfaction guarantee to ensure every student feels at home, backed by a professional team that monitors and responds to inquiries to ensure every housing need is addressed as quickly as possible.
